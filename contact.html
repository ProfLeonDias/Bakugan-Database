Contact The Owner

If you would like to contact me for any reason, the primary and fastest way would be to join my Discord Server.


Bakugan: Infinite Dimensions is my public server for all things Bakugan related. It was primarily envisioned as a roleplay server, but also doubles as a central hub of all things Bakugan. Participating in the RP is not required, but anybody curious enough to try will be met with a friendly staff and active owner. Our server introduces custom rules and cards that perfected the rules established in the anime, with inspiration taken from the IRL TCG and Wii Game. We have a level-up system, a shop system, generous participation rewards, and we have even introduced Aurelus as a 7th playable attribute. My staff and I have spent months retooling and rebalancing every card and game mechanic in order to provide the fairest experience- this is its own exclusive metagame! You can play with Battle Brawlers or New Vestroia rules (Yes, we found a way to make this work!)


If you don't care about any of that or think the above paragraph is cringe, then you can still enjoy your time in there. Ask questions, share your own collections and findings, social media posts, or even partner your own Discord server with us. Most importantly, try to make some friends. You can even try your hand at selling or trading Bakugan, with the stipulation that the rules are the same as they are in Qubecove. If someone has been found to be a known scammer, they will be banned immediately and without chance to appeal. If you would like to join just to keep up with BakuganDB updates, there is a role and announcement channel specifically for this purpose. Oftentimes I will give a brief progress report once every couple of days.


If you lack Discord or don't care to use it much, you can still try your hand at contacting me via Instagram or Twitter/X, but the likelihood of my response is lower and will take much longer to hear a reply. I simply do not use these apps as often, but remain available as an alternative should you need them. Links are contained within the footer of each page.
